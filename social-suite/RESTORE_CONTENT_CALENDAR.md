# Restore Marketing — 30-Day Content Calendar (v1)

30 reels across 30 days — one clear post per day, mapped to a template,
a hook, a vertical, a CTA keyword, and a post-time. Designed to teach
the IG/TT algorithms which of the 4 verticals we belong to WITHOUT
diluting the account by mixing verticals in a single video.

## Vertical rotation rules
Each week hits all four verticals but leans on the account's home base:
- **Agency (home base)** — 40% of posts
- **Local biz lead-gen** — 25%
- **Reputation / restoration** — 20%
- **Ecom / DTC** — 15%

If Restore's actual client mix skews, rebalance week-by-week.

## Posting times (local ET)
Data from 2026 IG/TT engagement patterns for B2B/agency accounts:
- **Weekday sweet spots:** 7:30–8:30am, 12:00–1:00pm, 6:00–7:30pm
- **Weekend:** 9:00–11:00am (algo prefers content it can push all day)
- Never post the same reel to IG and TT the same day — 48h stagger.

## Format legend
- **T#** = template ID from `RESTORE_VIDEO_TEMPLATES.md`
- **H###** = hook ID from `RESTORE_HOOK_LIBRARY.md`
- **KW** = CTA keyword (matches magnet in `RESTORE_CAPTION_TEMPLATES.md`)

## Weekly ops rhythm
- **Monday 7:30am** — publish + reply to all Sunday DMs.
- **Tuesday 4pm** — batch-shoot day 1 of next 5 reels.
- **Wednesday 10am** — screen-record teardown captures for T3/T13.
- **Thursday 3pm** — edit + queue Fri/Sat/Sun.
- **Friday 9am** — analytics review; mark winners in the hook library.
- **Weekend** — light — DM triage + community management only.

## Content bank (batch this in one session)
Producing this calendar needs:
- **20 hook-only takes** (7–10s each) — for T1, T2, T15.
- **15 body chunks** (10–15s each) — for the middle of every reel.
- **10 CTA takes** (one per keyword) — reusable across templates.
- **5 confession cold-opens** on phone camera — for T11.
- **8 screen-records** — 4 GBP, 2 Meta Ads Manager, 2 Klaviyo/Shopify.
- **2 testimonial recordings** — client-side Zoom, kept ≤3 min per.

Total shoot time: one 2.5-hour founder session + async screen-recording
sessions.

## Fallback plan (missing a day)
If a shoot slips: pull from the hook library, record a 20-sec T1 or T14
on your phone, use one CTA take, publish same day. Consistency > polish.
Never skip a day in the first 30 — the algo learns cadence in week 3.
