# Restore Marketing — Video Template Pack (v1)

15 concrete, copy-ready templates for Restore Marketing reels. Each one:
- **Verticals:** which of the 4 Restore verticals it fits (Agency, Local,
  Reputation, Ecom).
- **Length:** target duration.
- **Hook:** the exact opening 1.5s (verbal + on-screen text — different).
- **Beats:** timestamped shot/beat list.
- **Shot list:** what to actually film / screen-record.
- **CTA:** the last-3s ask + keyword.
- **Caption pattern:** what goes on the post.

Pair every template with the matching hook family from
`RESTORE_HOOK_LIBRARY.md` and the caption skeleton from
`RESTORE_CAPTION_TEMPLATES.md`.

---

## Master frame (all templates)
**Hook (0–1.5s) → Context (1.5–5s) → Body (5–end-5s) → Payoff (end-5s to end-3s) → CTA (last 3s).** Close with a line that loops back to the hook (bumps rewatch 15–30%).

## T1 — Contrarian Strike (short talking-head)
- **Verticals:** All 4. Default template.
- **Length:** 15–22s.
- **Hook (0–1.5s):**
  - Voice: "Everyone tells you to post daily. That's why your engagement is dying."
  - On-screen text: `POST DAILY IS BROKEN.`
- **Beats:**
  - 0–1.5s hook (medium close-up, cold open, no intro).
  - 1.5–3.5s the consensus you're breaking, one sentence.
  - 3.5–7s the actual reason it's wrong (specific mechanism).
  - 7–17s your alternative — one specific move, one number.
  - 17–19s payoff line — "here's what happens when you do it."
  - 19–22s CTA + loop-back to hook.
- **Shot list:** 1 talking-head take. Cut it into 4–5 jump-cuts on breaths. Add 1 punch-in on the "number" beat. B-roll on any noun (dashboard, DM inbox, calendar).
- **CTA:** "Comment SYSTEM — I'll DM you the exact posting cadence."
- **Caption skeleton:** `HOOK LINE. \n\n Consensus. Reason it's wrong. Alt move. \n\n Comment SYSTEM — I'll DM you the cadence doc.`

## T2 — Hormozi Bold Claim Stack
- **Verticals:** Agency, Ecom.
- **Length:** 8–15s.
- **Hook:**
  - Voice: "The biggest mistake agency owners make in year one."
  - On-screen text: `YEAR-ONE AGENCY KILLER →`
- **Beats:**
  - 0–1.5s hook.
  - 1.5–4s reason 1 (2–3s, tight).
  - 4–6s reason 2.
  - 6–8s reason 3.
  - 8–12s the one-line lesson that ties them together.
  - 12–15s CTA.
- **Shot list:** single medium close-up. Yellow-highlight subtitles with the money word. Whoosh SFX between reasons. Optional: numbered `01 / 02 / 03` on-screen chip in top-right corner.
- **CTA:** "Comment PLAYBOOK — I'll DM you the full breakdown."
- **Caption skeleton:** three-bullet stack in caption, then CTA.

## T3 — Screen-Record Teardown
- **Verticals:** Agency, Local, Ecom.
- **Length:** 25–40s.
- **Hook:**
  - Voice: "This Google Business Profile is losing this contractor $8k/month. Watch."
  - On-screen text: `$8K/MO LEAK. FOUND IT.`
- **Beats:**
  - 0–1.5s hook over a full-screen GBP screenshot (or Meta Ads Manager, or a landing page).
  - 1.5–4s zoom-punch to the specific flaw. Red circle-highlight (Restore signal-red `#E63946`).
  - 4–15s cut to talking head — name the mechanism. One sentence per problem.
  - 15–25s cut back to screen showing the fix. Green checkmark overlay.
  - 25–30s payoff: "Fix in 30 seconds. Adds an average of X calls/week."
  - 30–35s CTA + loop-back to hook.
- **Shot list:** OBS/QuickTime screen record of real (blurred) example, then talking-head B-roll. Highlight tool: rounded rectangles animated in with 200ms scale.
- **CTA:** "Comment AUDIT — I'll do a free 3-min Loom of yours."
- **Caption skeleton:** describe the leak. Free-audit CTA.

## T4 — Case Study Reveal ($X → $Y)
- **Verticals:** All 4.
- **Length:** 30–45s.
- **Hook:**
  - Voice: "$3k retainer → $18k retainer. Same client. Here's what changed."
  - On-screen text: `$3K → $18K. SAME CLIENT.`
- **Beats:**
  - 0–1.5s hook — split-screen or before/after cards, 0.5s each.
  - 1.5–4s who / what / constraint (one sentence).
  - 4–20s the ONE decision that caused the lift (specific move — reposition, add asset, change contract, restructure funnel).
  - 20–30s the outcome numbers (revenue, calls, ROAS, review count — with on-screen chart or screenshot).
  - 30–40s generalize: "You can do this if [criteria]."
  - 40–45s CTA + loop-back.
- **Shot list:** talking-head + 2 screen-record inserts (before dashboard, after dashboard). Real numbers, blur client name.
- **CTA:** "Comment SCALE — I'll DM you the exact reposition script."
- **Caption:** headline the $X → $Y, then the mechanism in 2 lines.

## T6 — Identity Callout + Reframe
- **Verticals:** All 4.
- **Length:** 20–28s.
- **Hook:**

  - On-screen text: `SOLO AGENCY. HOURLY. -40%.`
- **Beats:**

  - 4–15s the reframe: value-based pricing or performance pricing. One example.
  - 15–22s the specific line to say when a prospect asks "how much?"
  - 22–28s CTA + loop-back.
- **Shot list:** talking-head + B-roll of a proposal doc / Stripe dashboard.
- **CTA:** "Comment PRICE — I'll DM you my 3-tier proposal template."
- **Caption:** headline + the line to say + CTA.

## T7 — 3-Signs Diagnostic (Rule of Three)
- **Verticals:** All 4.
- **Length:** 22–30s.
- **Hook:**
  - Voice: "3 signs your ad account is being throttled — and nobody told you."
  - On-screen text: `3 SIGNS YOU'RE THROTTLED. →`
- **Beats:**
  - 0–1.5s hook.
  - 1.5–2s number chip `01` appears top-right.
  - 2–8s sign 1 — name it, prove it (screen or number).
  - 8–9s number chip flips to `02`.
  - 9–15s sign 2.
  - 15–22s sign 3 (make this the strongest — retention curve peaks here).
  - 22–27s the fix + CTA + loop-back.
- **Shot list:** talking head + 3 screen-record B-roll clips + 3 number-chip animations.
- **CTA:** "Comment AUDIT — I'll pull yours in 3 min."
- **Caption:** the 3 signs listed, then CTA.

## T10 — "If I Started Today" Playbook
- **Verticals:** Agency (primary).
- **Length:** 45–60s (case-study length — high open loop).
- **Hook:**
  - Voice: "If I had to start a marketing agency today with $0, here's exactly what I'd do."
  - On-screen text: `RESTART AN AGENCY. $0. →`
- **Beats:**
  - 0–1.5s hook.
  - 1.5–3s "the entire playbook is 5 moves. Watch to #5 — it's the one nobody does."
  - 3–12s move 1 (niche selection).
  - 12–20s move 2 (offer).
  - 20–30s move 3 (lead source).
  - 30–40s move 4 (delivery / SOP).
  - 40–52s move 5 (retention / referral loop) — payload.
  - 52–58s CTA + loop-back.
- **Shot list:** talking head + 5 numbered B-roll cards. Jump-cut between each of the 5.
- **CTA:** "Comment RESTART — I'll DM you the full 5-move doc."
- **Caption:** headline + the 5 moves as a caption list + CTA.

## T11 — Cold-Open Confession
- **Verticals:** All 4.
- **Length:** 25–35s.
- **Hook:**
  - Voice: "I burned $47k on ads for one client before I figured out why nothing was converting."
  - On-screen text: `$47K WASTED. HERE'S WHY.`
- **Beats:**
  - 0–1.5s hook (raw phone-camera cold open — not the polished tripod look).
  - 1.5–5s specifically what you tried.
  - 5–20s the mistake — one root cause (offer, tracking, audience).
  - 20–30s what you'd do differently. One transferable rule.
  - 30–35s CTA + loop-back.
- **Shot list:** phone-camera selfie (cold open), then cut to standard tripod for the reveal. The format swap **is** the pattern interrupt.
- **CTA:** "Comment LEARN — I'll DM you the pre-flight checklist."
- **Caption:** the confession → the rule → CTA.

## How to pick a template
| Situation | Template |
|---|---|
| Fresh account, need first 10 posts | T1, T7, T15, T14 — high identity + rule-of-three |
| Client win just closed | T4, T12 — case study / testimonial |
| Bad ad account / bad GBP in the wild | T3, T5 — teardown / restoration |
| Industry news hit today | T8 — green-screen react (post within 24h) |
| Founder wants face-of-brand time | T14, T11 — founder-led |
| Cold audience, need saves | T2, T7, T10 — list / playbook / stack |
| Warmer audience, need DMs | T5, T6, T9 — pain + specific CTA |
| Ecom-vertical week | T13 primary, T3 backup |

## What to film in a single 2-hour session
Batch shoot to get **10 reels' worth of talking-head footage** in one
sitting:
1. Set up the framing once (medium close-up, off-axis key, styled
   background).
2. Rip through **10 hooks in a row**, back-to-back, resetting only
   between takes. 2 takes each.
3. For each hook, film **3 short body chunks** (10s each) with the
   answer/proof/story.
4. Film **10 CTAs in a row** — one per keyword (AUDIT, SYSTEM, PLAYBOOK,
   FIX, SCALE, LEADS, CASE, TEARDOWN, ONBOARD, LOCAL).
5. Film **5 confession cold-opens** on the PHONE (not tripod) — for T11.

Editor then assembles reels post-session, plugging any hook/body/CTA
into any template. This is the "60 reels/session" agency method.
