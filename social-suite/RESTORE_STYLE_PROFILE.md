# Restore Marketing — Clip Style Profile

Sibling to `STYLE_PROFILE.md` (HP Landscaping). This one governs the
**Restore Marketing** brand ONLY — an agency serving all four verticals:
full-service digital marketing, local-biz lead-gen, reputation/presence
restoration, and ecommerce/DTC growth.

## Brand identity (locked)
- **Name:** Restore Marketing
- **Positioning:** the agency that **restores** dead marketing —
  dormant socials, dead Google profiles, stalled ad accounts, broken
  funnels, buried review pages, cold retention curves. The verb is the
  brand.
- **Voice:** operator, not guru. Blunt, specific, numbers-first, no
  fake urgency. Founder-led. Confident but not smug.
- **Verticals we speak to:** local service businesses (contractors, home
  services, med-spa, restaurants), ecom/DTC, small B2B, personal brands.
- **Never says:** "growth hacks," "10x your revenue," "in this economy,"
  "the algorithm hates you," "boost your engagement," emoji-stuffed hype.
- **Always shows:** the exact move, the exact number, the exact screen.

## Platforms (Phase 1)
Instagram Reels + TikTok (9:16, 15–30s primary; up to 45s for case
studies) + Facebook Reels + Facebook feed. Same master export re-used
across all four; different captions per platform.

## ⭐ LOCKED VISUAL RECIPE — Restore Marketing (v1, review after 30 posts)

### Aspect + specs
- Vertical 1080×1920, 30fps, H.264, ≤50 Mbps.
- Top safe zone 250px, bottom safe zone 500px. Anchor text in the
  middle 900px band.
- Export 2 sizes: master 1080×1920 for IG/TT/FB Reels; 1080×1350 for
  FB feed carry-over.

### Talking-head framing
- Medium close-up — top of chest to just above head.
- Eye-line just above camera (camera at forehead height).
- **Small headroom** — eyes at ~1/3 down from top. Never center the head.
- Key light off-axis 30–45°. **No ring light** (kills the frame).
- Background: bookshelf, plants, or styled desk clutter. **Never** an
  empty white wall — reads as low-effort in 2026.

### Subtitles
- Style: **word-by-word or 2–3 word chunks**, pop-in animated per word.
- Font: **Montserrat Bold** primary; **Anton** as accent for hooks and
  big numbers. Both live in `/mnt/skills/.../canvas-fonts/`.
- Color: **white with 3px black outline**, one word per sentence
  highlighted in **Restore accent yellow `#F5C518`**.
- Position: lower-middle third, ~35% up from bottom.
- Animation: scale 0.9 → 1.0 in ~80ms per word. No slow fades.
- CapCut preset name to search: "Hormozi Style" or "Karaoke Captions."
- Never the default CapCut sans — reads amateur.

### Logo + brand mark
- **Restore logo top-right corner** on every clip, semi-transparent
  (60% opacity), ~130px wide, 30px margin. Needed asset:
  `content/brand/restore-logo.png` — owner still to drop.
- **Outro end-card** (crossfade in over the last 1.5s):
  `content/brand/restore-outro.mp4` — animated Restore logo + accent
  glow + phone/URL/handle + "Marketing that pays for itself." tagline.
  Asset to build.

### Color system
- **Restore accent yellow:** `#F5C518` (subtitle highlight, callouts,
  underlines).
- **Deep navy:** `#0F1D2E` (backgrounds, lower-third bar).
- **Off-white:** `#F4F1EA` (text on navy).
- **Signal red:** `#E63946` (used ONLY for "problem" / "wrong" callouts
  — never for CTAs).
- No gradients on text. Flat only.

### Cut rhythm
- **Visual change every 1.2–2.0s** — cut, zoom-punch, text swap, B-roll
  insert, color pop, or scale shift.
- Practical: **5–8 changes per 10s** for talking-head; **8–12** for
  screen-record teardowns.
- Kill silences >250ms. Jump-cut on breath.
- Punch-in (10% zoom) or B-roll every ~4s.

### Transitions
- Whip-pan (direction-matched, motion blur) between locations/POVs.
- Match-cut for teardowns (mouse motion → hand motion, etc.).
- Freeze-frame + label ("this is the mistake") for emphasis.
- Zoom-punch (5–15%) on the **noun** of the emphasized word.
- **No fancy transitions** (star wipes, page turns, etc.) — reads dated.

### B-roll rules
- Any spoken noun with visual form gets 0.6–1.2s of B-roll — "dashboard,"
  "email," "invoice," "review," "ad account."
- Every spoken number gets an on-screen text overlay + subtle whoosh SFX.
- Build a tagged library in `content/broll/restore/` — screen recordings
  of GBP, Meta Ads Manager, GA4, Klaviyo, ManyChat, Google Search, plus
  hands-on-keyboard, dashboard sweeps, testimonial cuts.

### Audio
- **B2B accounts get throttled on viral consumer sounds** — stick to
  categories, not hits.
- Working categories: ambient piano / lo-fi cinematic; cinematic
  build-ups (0–8s ramp, hit on payoff); upbeat corporate acoustic;
  percussive minimal (claps + snaps).
- **Original audio** (your own voice) is favored by Reels for B2B —
  publish clean talking-head, then reuse it as your own trending pool.
- Whoosh / riser SFX layered at cut on every emphasis.
- How to find a trending sound: IG Reels → audio icon → Trending tab.
  Look for the ↑ arrow. Use-count velocity <10k AND rising = early;
  >500k = late. Post within 3–5 days of saving.

## Hard rules (do every time)
- **Hook resolves in ≤1.5s.** Voice hook by 1.2s; on-screen hook text
  swaps by 1.5s. On-screen text and voice should **NOT match
  word-for-word** — mismatch increases dwell.
- **Length target: 28–45s** for talking-head; **15–25s** for punchy
  hook + one payoff; **45–60s** only if payoff is dense; **60–90s**
  reserved for case studies with strong open loops.
- **Loop-back close** — end on a line that connects to the opening hook.
  Bumps rewatch rate 15–30%.
- **One CTA per video.** Multiple CTAs cut action rate ~40%.
- Any spoken number gets an on-screen overlay + SFX.
- Every session-source clip goes through the queue with `brand: "restore"`
  — a Restore clip can never post to HP accounts, enforced upstream.
- **Never use flubs / mess-ups / botched parts.**
- **No subtitles?** Wrong — Restore uses subtitles (unlike HP). Reason:
  most agency-content viewers scroll muted; HP's audience taps sound on.
- **No burned CTA** on top of a talking mouth. Text stays lower third.
- **Vertical 1080×1920** always. Never crop 16:9 footage — 3-panel
  vertical stack instead (HP's trick, we reuse for agency content).

## Voice / caption hard rules
- Hook line first (max 8 words). Line break. Then the promise. Line
  break. Then the CTA. Line break. Then the hashtag block.
- **Numbers > adjectives.** "Booked 34 calls in 11 days" beats "insane
  results." Always.
- **One-line CTA** using a keyword the DM auto-reply catches:
  **AUDIT, PLAYBOOK, SOP, TEMPLATE, SYSTEM, LEADS, SCALE, FUNNEL, GROW**
  — never LINK / INFO / YES (fail).
- CTA format template: `Comment {KEYWORD} — I'll DM you the {asset}.`
- Never "link in bio" as sole CTA on a Reel. Bio-link is fallback only.
- Sign-off (rotate): `— Restore` / `— the Restore team` / (nothing).
- **No emoji stuffing.** Max 2 per caption; skip if unsure. Approved
  set: ✅ 📈 🎯 💬 📌 🔎 🧠 ⚡ 🛠 📞.
- Phone / handle: DM the account. No public phone number on Restore's
  posts (agency, not local service).

## Niche cheat sheet — the four Restore verticals
When picking a template or hook, know which of these you're speaking to:

| Vertical | Pain we lead with | Proof they need to see | CTA asset |
|---|---|---|---|
| Full-service agency owners | Time & margin — running the shop | SOPs, dashboards, retainer stubs | SOP, SYSTEM |
| Local biz lead-gen (contractors, home svc) | Phone not ringing | GBP screenshots, review counts, call logs | AUDIT, LEADS |
| Reputation / presence restoration | Dead Google profile, 2-star average | Before/after profile screenshots, review lifts | AUDIT, FIX |
| Ecom / DTC | ROAS collapse, retention curve | Klaviyo flows, ad account, GA4 revenue chart | TEARDOWN, PLAYBOOK |

Every clip picks exactly ONE vertical. Do not mix in one video — dilutes
the hook and confuses the algorithm about who to show it to.

## Publishing cadence
- **4–7 Reels/week** per account for algo learning phase.
- Reuse strongest 20% as paid boost within 14 days.
- Cross-post to TikTok / FB Reels 48h apart (never same-day — duplicate
  suppression).
- Kill any video below **45% avg-watch** after 72h — don't try to save
  it with paid, signal is signal.

## Winning recipe (source of every template in `RESTORE_VIDEO_TEMPLATES.md`)
1. **Hook in first ~1.5s** — one of the 12 hook types in the templates doc.
2. **28–45s completion window** (unless case study).
3. **Cut / motion every 1.2–2s.**
4. **On-screen text ≠ voice.**
5. **One specific number, one specific move.**
6. **One CTA, keyword-based, DM-catchable.**
7. **Loop-back close** to the opening hook.
