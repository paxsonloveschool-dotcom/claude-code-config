# HP Landscaping — Viral Reveal-Reel Playbook (Late 2025 / 2026)

Hook-first transformation content for TikTok / Reels / Shorts. Editor-ready.

## Governing rule
Platforms score completion rate + replays from frame one. Swipe in the first ~1.5s = throttled.
Optimize: stop the scroll instantly → hold to payoff → make it loop. Target **7–15s** (≈68%
completion under 15s vs 48% over 60s; up to 1.8× replays). 21s only if the transformation earns it.

## 1. Hook (first 1–3s) — priority #1
- Open on the payoff (0.5–1s flash of the finished reveal, drone push-in), then HARD CUT to the ugly before.
- First frame MUST carry motion (drone push-in / whip-zoom / subject walking).
- Hook text BURNED IN on frame one (never rely on caption). 1–2 short lines.
- Hook lines that perform (pick tension, not brag):
  - "THIS WAS A MUD PIT 3 WEEKS AGO"
  - "They said this backyard was a lost cause."
  - "$85K. Watch what that actually buys you."
  - "POV: you finally called a real landscaper."
  - "Wait for the drone shot at the end."
  - "Nobody believes this is the same yard."
  - Numbers/specifics ("3 weeks", "612 sq ft", "$85K") out-hold vague hooks.

## 2. Structure & pacing
- 15s target, 21s ceiling. Change something on screen every 1–2s (cut, punch-in, new text line, wipe).
- 8–14 cuts in 15s (~0.7–1/sec), accelerating toward the reveal, timed to the beat.
- Spine: 0–2s hook (flash-forward + promise) → 2–4s worst "before" → 4–11s fast build montage
  (demo, excavation, pavers, sod, water fill; speed-ramp boring parts) → 11–15s full reveal held
  longest (2–3s) on the audio drop. Never show the full finished result until the final beat.

## 3. On-screen text
- Heavy condensed sans, ALL CAPS: **Anton / Bebas Neue / Montserrat Black**, thick black stroke,
  yellow highlight on the key word (Hormozi preset).
- Hold 2–4 word chunks ~600–900ms each (machine-gun captions now read cheap).
- Lower-middle third (~10–15% height); hook line higher/centered.
- Big number/price callouts near the payoff: "$85,000", "3 WEEKS", "612 SQ FT", "18 TONS OF STONE."
- Emoji minimal (one functional 🤯/➡️).

## 4. Audio
- Pick one engine: (a) satisfying nat-sound/ASMR (pavers clack, water rush, sod thump) — highest
  completion; or (b) trending track with the beat drop ON the reveal. Best: track underneath, duck
  it and pop nat-sound for 1–2 satisfying moments, swell back for the drop. Assume muted viewing —
  burned-in captions carry the story.

## 5. Retention tricks
- Open loop / "wait for it" — withhold the full reveal to the last beat.
- Design the last frame to flow into the first (visual rhyme) so it loops.
- Progress cue ("DAY 1 → DAY 21" / step counter).
- Front-load one satisfying beat ~3–5s, save the biggest for the reveal.

## 6. Caption / hashtag / posting
- Caption: 1–2 lines + a 3-word-answerable question to bait comments.
- Hashtags 5–15, three tiers: broad (#landscaping #outdoorliving #hardscape), niche (#pavers
  #poolbuild #sodinstall), local (#[City]Landscaper). Local matters most. Always add location tag.
- Reply to every comment in the first 60 min. Post as a series ("Backyard Build Ep. 7").
- Track DMs / profile visits / quote requests, not follower count.

## Recommended shot-sequence template (15–21s hook-first reveal)
| Time | Shot | Text (Anton/Bebas caps, yellow accent) | Audio |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.0–1.0 | Drone flash-forward over finished space → hard cut | "THIS WAS A **MUD PIT** 3 WEEKS AGO" | track starts / nat sting |
| 1.0–3.5 | Worst before angle, slow push-in | "612 SQ FT. TOTAL DISASTER." | music low, ambient |
| 3.5–5.0 | First satisfying beat (demo/excavation), speed-ramp | "DAY 1 →" | pop nat-sound |
| 5.0–10.0 | Build montage: pavers/base/sod, 5–7 fast beats on beat | "18 TONS OF STONE", "$85K BUILD" | music building |
| 10.0–13.0 | Water fills / final piece drops | "WAIT FOR IT…" | beat rising |
| 13.0–18.0 | Full golden-hour drone reveal, held 3s (mirrors opener) | "SAME. BACKYARD." | BEAT DROP |
| 18.0–21.0 | Slow orbit on hero → loops to frame one | "[City] — booking spring builds 👇" | outro |

Editor priorities: (1) burned-in hook text frame one, (2) motion in first frame, (3) withhold full
reveal to last beat, (4) beat-synced ~1s cuts, (5) loop last frame into first, (6) big number/price
callouts, (7) heavy-condensed captions held 600–900ms.
