This Privacy Policy describes how **[BUSINESS NAME]** ("we," "us") handles
information in connection with our internal content-scheduling application (the
"App"), which we use to publish our own marketing content to our own social
media accounts.

## Who this applies to
The App is an internal tool operated by [BUSINESS NAME] to manage our own social
media presence. We are the only operator and intended user. We do not offer the
App as a service to third parties or the general public.

## Information we process
- **Social account credentials/tokens.** When we connect our own accounts
  (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, X, LinkedIn), the platforms provide
  access tokens that let the App publish on our behalf. We store these securely
  and use them only to publish our content.
- **Content we create.** Videos, images, captions, and scheduling details that
  we upload or generate for posting.
- **Files from our own cloud storage.** Media we place in our own Dropbox folder
  for processing.
- **Operational logs.** Timestamps and status of posts for troubleshooting.

We do **not** collect personal information from the audiences who view our posts,
beyond what the social platforms themselves provide to their account owners.

## How we use information
- To schedule, format, caption, and publish our own content to our own accounts.
- To generate captions/hooks using an AI provider (Anthropic) — only the content
  we choose to process is sent for this purpose.
- To monitor that posts succeed and to debug failures.

## How information is shared
- **With the social platforms** you connect, solely to publish your content,
  subject to their terms and policies.
- **With service providers** strictly to operate the App: Anthropic (AI caption
  generation) and Dropbox (our media storage). We do not sell or rent any data.

## Data retention & deletion
We retain tokens and content only as long as needed to operate the App. You can
disconnect any account at any time, which revokes the App's access. To request
deletion of stored data, contact us at the address below.

## Security
We protect access tokens and credentials using industry-standard measures
(encryption at rest/in transit, restricted access). No method is 100% secure,
but we take reasonable steps to safeguard information.

## Your platform rights
Account access is governed by each platform's terms (Meta, TikTok, Google/
YouTube, X, LinkedIn). You may revoke the App's access through each platform's
settings at any time.

## Children
The App is not directed to children and does not knowingly process children's
data.

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date reflects the latest version.
