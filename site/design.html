faqs

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our process, services, or how we work? Here are the answers to the most common things clients ask us.

01
How long does the landscape design process take?
Most projects take one to three weeks, depending on scope, property size, design complexity, feedback rounds, and final client approvals.
02
Do you provide 3D renderings?
Yes, we provide detailed 3D renderings that help visualize layouts, materials, lighting, and spatial flow before construction begins.
03
Can I request revisions to my design?
Yes, we include revision rounds to refine layouts, materials, and features until your final design fully meets expectations.
04
Is construction included with the design service?
Design services are separate from construction, but all plans are fully build-ready for smooth execution by our installation teams.
05
What information do you need to get started?
We need site photos, property measurements, inspirational references, functional requirements, and budget goals to craft a tailored design plan.
06
Do you design for residential and commercial projects?
We design for residential homes and commercial properties, delivering functional, beautiful outdoor environments tailored to each project’s purpose.
07
How do I book a design consultation?
Simply book a free consultation online, and our team will contact you to schedule your personalized design discovery session.
