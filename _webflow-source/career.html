charity

Our giving

At Higher Purpose Landscaping, we believe our mission goes far beyond yards and outdoor spaces. We are honored to use our work as a platform to serve, to give, and to impact lives around the world. Partnering with ministries, nonprofits, and first responders allows us to share in a greater purpose — one of compassion, generosity, and hope.

Here are some of the organizations and initiatives we are privileged to support:

Brazos Valley Gives

Brazos Valley Gives brings the region together as one community to raise critical funding and awareness for nonprofits throughout the Brazos Valley.

Brazos Valley Younglife

Focused on helping people of all abilities to Jesus Christ and help them grow in their faith.

The Bryan/College Station Chamber of Commerce

The Bryan/College Station Chamber of Commerce exists to enhance the economic well-being of the businesses in the community it serves.

Kerrville Fire Department – July Flood Response

In July’s historic flooding and chaos, we partnered with Kerrville Fire Department to provide resources and support for first responders serving around the clock to protect families and property.

Flooding Relief

Hands-on disaster response during devastating flooding. Our team and partners assisted families by helping with cleanup, rebuilding efforts, and immediate aid in the aftermath of the storms.

Camp Kesem

A national nonprofit that provides free summer camps and year-round support for children whose parents are affected by cancer. These camps give kids a chance to heal, connect, and find joy in a safe, nurturing environment.

Kids Across America

A Christian sports camp ministry dedicated to transforming the lives of urban youth and their mentors. Through faith, athletics, and leadership development, it equips young people to become leaders in their communities.

Bright Hill Collective

The Bright Hill Collective is a Christian nonprofit organization operating in El Salvador that focuses on Spiritual Renewal, Education, and Construction to support the local community.

Matthew 10 Project (Pakistan)

Partnering to bring freedom to modern-day slaves trapped in brick kilns in Pakistan. This ministry provides rescue, rehabilitation, and sustainable solutions to break generational cycles of bondage.

CRU Ministry – China, Japan, East Asia

CRU’s global outreach supports students and families in East Asia by offering discipleship, leadership development, and life-changing spiritual resources.

College Station Fire Department

Our local heroes who stand ready every day to respond to emergencies. We are proud to support the firefighters who safeguard the Brazos Valley community.

EPIC Movement (CRU Ministry)

A campus ministry under CRU that empowers Asian American college students to grow in faith, develop leadership, and impact their campuses for Christ.

Bryan YoungLife

We’ve had the privilege of giving to Bryan YoungLife, a ministry devoted to walking alongside high school students and showing them the love of Jesus in a real and approachable way.

A&M Consolidated FFA

We proudly sponsor A&M Consolidated FFA because of the incredible impact it has in preparing students for the real world.

Kanakuk Institute

The Kanakuk Institute is an eight-month college graduate discipleship and leadership program equipping students with the truth of God's Word.

Active Missions in Indonesia

Serving some of the most remote communities in Indonesia by bringing clean water, education, and the message of hope. These missions focus on meeting practical needs while sharing faith in action.

Active Missions in China

Partnering with underground ministries that encourage faith, provide discipleship training, and deliver resources in regions where open expression of Christianity is restricted.

Active Missions in Zambia

Investing in education, medical support, and church development to strengthen communities. These missions help empower local leaders and equip families with the tools to thrive.

Active Missions in North Africa

Focused on humanitarian aid, discipleship, and community building in one of the most challenging mission fields. These efforts bring hope and essential resources to families in need.
