Rejoicing in Freedom in Pakistan!

A Matthew 10 International team traveled to Pakistan to support local churches and free families trapped in brick-kiln slavery. By God’s grace, 73 families were set free during this trip.

The story highlights Faiz, a 75-year-old man who endured decades of abuse, loss, and generational slavery, yet chose faith, worship, and joy in Jesus. After 63 years in bondage, Faiz and his family were finally declared free.

The trip concluded with a Freedom Celebration, worshiping alongside families newly released from slavery—testimonies of restored lives, strengthened faith, and generational change through Christ.
