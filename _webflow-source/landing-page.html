Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our process, services, or how we work? Here are the answers to the most common things clients ask us.

01
What does “luxury landscaping” actually include?
Luxury landscape design combines custom planning, premium materials, precision construction, and personalized service, from luxury residential hardscape services to integrated outdoor landscape lighting design and water feature installation.
02
How do your designs reflect both form and function?
Every design prioritizes flow, comfort, and longevity while maintaining visual harmony. Beauty is built into every structural detail.
03
Do you work with designers, architects, or builders?
Yes. Our team provides complete in-house design using 3D landscape design services, CAD layouts, and master planning, and for clients searching for “custom pool builders in College Station TX”, we also collaborate seamlessly with architects or builders as needed.
04
What is the typical investment range for a full outdoor transformation?
Projects typically range from $50,000 to $300,000+, depending on scope, materials, and complexity, and for homeowners searching for outdoor kitchen design and build contractors in College Station we deliver luxury-tier quality with transparent pricing.
05
Do you handle everything from start to finish?
Yes. We are a true outdoor living space build & design company in College Station, managing everything from demolition to final landscape lighting installation, so when clients search for a landscape lighting designer near me, they can trust our team to handle every detail in-house.
06
How long do your projects take?
Timelines vary based on scope. Most large-scale projects are completed within 4 - 10 weeks, weather permitting. We provide clear timelines and weekly updates.
07
How do you communicate during the project?
Clients receive consistent communication from our project manager, along with daily text/photo updates and milestone check-ins.
08
What guarantees or warranties do you provide?
We offer a 1-year craftsmanship warranty on all structural work. Extended warranties are available on select systems and materials.
